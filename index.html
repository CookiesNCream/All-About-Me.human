Simply-Me

It is nice to be part of the GitHub community! Here is how I would like to introduce myself. Hi! I'm Linda, a Technical Advisor / Consultant at InVINCIble Enterprises. I'm motivated by my passion to creatively enhance the way users interact with computing devices because some form of user interface is utilized for such interactions. I wear many hats, from IT training, research, support, guiding, and inspiring, to user experience interviews, heuristic evaluation, scenario-based evaluation, usability testing, wireframing, prototyping, front-end and back-end development, server administration, project management, quantitative and qualitative data analysis, and technology evangelism. When I'm not working, I enjoy reading articles about technology and general knowledge over cups of coffee.

I'm extremely passionate about innovation and invention across different disciplines, and enjoy collaborating with others in a variety of projects as much as receiving feedback and learning from others' viewpoints. I enjoy learning and applying new things, and am always dabbling with tools and breathing in new knowledge that are accessible to me. As such, I love solving problems, especially those without obvious answers, because when I do find answers, it is humbling, challenging, fulfilling, and exhilarating!

I'm enthusiastic about learning new things and applying it to projects to provide the best user experience to end users and enhance my learning experience.
